MCTA International School
MA 69/2, 53rd St, Mandalay
Contact: 09400494856
MCTA offers the education from K2 to iGCSE. It was established in 2005 as the first Singapore International School in Mandalay, dedicated to providing high quality international school education to the best and brightest young people in the region. Certified by the Myanmar Ministry of Education, the school is managed by Singapore-based RVi International. Known as one of Mandalay’s leading schools, MCTA is a progressive and dynamic institution with a reputation for quality teaching excellence and parent/student satisfaction.
 Chan Thar (Myanmar School)
MA 69/2, 53rd St, Mandalay
Contact: 09404757983
Chan Thar (Myanmar school programme of MCTA) offers the education from KG to Grade 12. Not only the goverment school student can attend, also international student can join as part time students. For part time students, there will be two classes from Mon to Fri and a whole day class on Saturday. Certified by the Myanmar Ministry of Education, the school is managed by Singapore-based RVi International.
